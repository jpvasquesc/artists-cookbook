Denise Levrtov's dessert

Invented by Denise Levertov. No name attached.

Steps

  1. Mix
    equal quantities of:
    Sour cream
    Tart applesauce
    Mashed bananas

  2. Add:
    Maple syrup to taste
    (If you put too much,
    add a little lemon juice)

  3. Top
    with: Sliced bananas and walnuts
